Discover the Lake District

Nearby Highlights

The Scafell Hotel sits at the gateway to some of the Lake District's most iconic landscapes and outdoor adventures.

From gentle lakeside walks to challenging mountain hikes, there is something for every visitor to enjoy.

Scafell Pike

England's highest mountain, rising just beyond the valley.

Borrowdale Valley

One of the most beautiful valleys in the Lake District, right outside the door.

Derwentwater

Tranquil waters, island views and lakeside walks a short drive away.

Keswick

A charming market town full of independent shops, cafés and local history.

Catbells

A famously rewarding fell walk with spectacular views over the lake.

Honister Pass

A dramatic mountain pass and slate mine at the head of the valley.

Your Lake District adventure starts here

Explore charming villages, cruise across tranquil waters, discover local history or simply take in the spectacular scenery that has inspired generations of visitors.
